THE MISSION

The Hunger Project is a global,non-profit organization committed to the sustainable end of world hunger. It strive for a sustainable solution to ending hunger based on the belief that hunger is more than food-it is about people.

A HOLYSTIC APPROACH

THP identifies three pillars to a sustainable solution to end hunger: start with women, mobilze communities,add engage gov. It adopts holistic approach,which dedicates to capacity building at the grassroots level.Resource are provided to impact beneficiaries skills, knowledge, and practice to end the poverty cycle.

IMPACT

Though a various of program and intiatives, THe hunger Project helps to build sustainable, long-term solution to hunger and poverty.

SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) aim to transform our world. They are a call to action to end poverty and inequality, protect the planet, and ensure that all people enjoy health, justice and prosperity. It is critical that no one is left behind.
